Ashanti's Video Editing Portfolio

Here are some of my favorite projects that I've worked on using Capcut. The videos were all created using clips from tv shows/movies that I downloaded off of youtube. I then imported the clips into capcut, rearranged them and then added music and transitions to match the vibe of that specific tv shows/movies. For example: two of the videos of remakes of tv show intros using clips from completely different shows.
